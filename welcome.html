Welcome

Easy practice Games on learning phonetic sounds from Quran.

Inshallah these Games will improve your Quran reading skills.

This WebApp Requires a modern web browser.

Tips

  • Each link from the side menu ( ) will help you study a page from Qaidah Noorania.
  • Press "Hear Again" button to hear the audio again
  • Select the correct choice A. B. or C. to move on
  • If you select wrong choice, nothing will happen
  • When your score reaches "needed score" then you are ready to move on
